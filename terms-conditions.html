Service Terms

These terms and conditions apply to all services provided by Sunshine Blinds Saddleworth ("we", "us", "our") to customers ("you", "your") in the Saddleworth area including Uppermill, Diggle, Delph, Greenfield, Dobcross, and Lydgate.

Free Consultations & Quotes

We offer free home consultations and quotes within our service area. Consultations are subject to availability and must be booked in advance. We reserve the right to charge a call-out fee for locations outside our standard service area.

Orders & Deposits

All orders require a deposit payment before manufacturing begins. The deposit amount will be clearly stated in your quote. Orders cannot be cancelled once manufacturing has commenced unless the products are faulty.

  • Standard deposit: 50% of total order value
  • Balance due on completion of installation
  • Payment methods accepted: Cash, card, bank transfer
  • All prices include VAT where applicable

Manufacturing & Delivery Times

Standard manufacturing time is 7-10 working days from order confirmation and deposit payment. Rush orders may be available at additional cost. Delivery and installation dates are estimates and may be subject to change due to circumstances beyond our control.

Installation Service

Professional installation is included in all quoted prices. We will:

  • Arrive at the agreed appointment time
  • Complete installation to professional standards
  • Clean up after installation
  • Demonstrate operation of your new blinds
  • Provide care and maintenance instructions

Warranty & Guarantees

We provide comprehensive warranties on all products and installation:

  • Product Warranty: 2 years on mechanisms, 1 year on fabrics
  • Installation Guarantee: 12 months on fitting and fixings
  • Satisfaction Guarantee: We'll return to address any issues at no charge

All measurements are taken by our trained professionals. We are responsible for measurement accuracy and will remake products at no charge if errors are due to incorrect measuring. Custom-made products cannot be returned unless faulty.
