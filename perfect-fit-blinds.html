How Perfect Fit Blinds Work

The clever design that's revolutionising window treatments

Ingenious Clip System

Each blind comes with specially designed clips that fit securely into the rubber beading around your UPVC window. The clips are nearly invisible and hold the blind frame firmly in place without any tools or fixings.

  • Clips into existing window beading
  • No tools required for fitting
  • Completely reversible
  • Works with most UPVC windows

Moves With Your Window

Because the blind is attached to the opening part of your window, it moves when you open the window. Perfect for tilt & turn windows common in newer Delph and Dobcross developments - you can ventilate whilst maintaining privacy.

  • Blind moves with opening windows
  • Perfect for tilt & turn windows
  • Maintains seal when window closed
  • No interference with window operation

Neat & Compact

Perfect Fit blinds sit neatly within your window recess, giving a clean, streamlined appearance. The slim profile means they don't protrude into your room - ideal for smaller Lydgate cottages or modern Greenfield apartments.

  • Sits flush within window recess
  • Minimal protrusion into room
  • Clean, contemporary appearance
  • Maximises window opening space

Perfect Fit Blinds FAQs

Everything you need to know about no-drill blinds

Perfect Fit blinds use a clever bracket system that clips directly onto your UPVC window frame. The brackets slide between the glass and the rubber gasket, holding the blind securely in place without any screws, drilling, or damage to your windows.

They're designed for UPVC windows and doors with a rubber gasket seal. Most modern double-glazed windows in Saddleworth homes are suitable. During our free consultation, we'll check your windows' compatibility - if they're not suitable, we'll recommend alternatives.
