Our Saddleworth Story

Over 20 years of bringing beautiful blinds to local families

Established 2003

We started our journey in 2003 with a simple mission: to bring quality window blinds and exceptional service to Saddleworth families. Over two decades later, we're still here, still local, and still passionate about what we do.

Truly Local

We live and work in the Saddleworth area. We know the unique character of homes from Victorian terraces in Uppermill to modern conversions in Diggle. This local knowledge means we understand exactly what works best for your home.

Family Values

We're a family business serving local families. We believe in honest advice, fair pricing, and treating every customer like a neighbour. When you call us, you'll speak to someone who genuinely cares about getting it right for you.
