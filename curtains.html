Interlinings

Tiebacks & Holdbacks

Valances & Pelmets

Add the finishing touch with matching valances or tailored pelmets. Perfect for concealing curtain tracks whilst adding architectural interest to your windows.

Pattern Matching

Expert pattern matching ensures designs flow seamlessly across curtain pairs. Essential for large patterns and crucial for the professional finish we guarantee.

Care & Maintenance

Full care instructions provided with every order. Many fabrics suitable for gentle home washing or professional cleaning to keep your curtains pristine for years.
