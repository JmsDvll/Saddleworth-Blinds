How Vision Blinds Work Their Magic

Simple to operate, stunning to look at

Open Position

When the sheer stripes align, you get a softly filtered view - perfect for enjoying Saddleworth's beautiful scenery whilst maintaining daytime privacy. Light floods in but passers-by can't see details inside.

  • Maintains your outside view
  • Filters harsh sunlight
  • Provides daytime privacy
  • Creates a light, airy feel

Closed Position

Offset the stripes and the opaque sections overlap completely, giving you full privacy and light control. In blackout versions, this creates near-complete darkness - ideal for bedrooms in Uppermill or media rooms in Greenfield.

  • Complete privacy day or night
  • Blocks unwanted light
  • Reduces outside noise
  • Helps with temperature control

Raised Position

Like a roller blind, vision blinds can be fully raised into a neat cassette at the top of your window. Perfect when you want maximum light or need to clean your windows - something we all do regularly in dusty Diggle!

  • Completely clear window
  • Neat cassette housing
  • Easy window access
  • Minimal stack height

Vision Blinds FAQs

Common questions about day & night blinds

Vision blinds (also called day & night blinds) feature two layers of fabric with alternating sheer and opaque stripes. By adjusting the alignment of these stripes, you can control privacy and light levels - from filtered daylight to complete privacy.

They offer more versatility than standard roller blinds. While roller blinds are either up or down, vision blinds let you fine-tune light and privacy throughout the day. They're perfect if you want the simplicity of roller blinds with the adjustability of venetians.

While vision blinds offer excellent privacy when closed, they don't provide 100% blackout due to the fabric construction. For bedrooms requiring complete darkness, we'd recommend our blackout roller blinds. Vision blinds are ideal for living areas where you want variable light control.

They're surprisingly easy to maintain! Regular dusting with a feather duster or vacuum brush attachment is usually sufficient. For deeper cleaning, most can be gently wiped with a damp cloth. The enclosed cassette design also helps keep dust off the mechanism.

Absolutely! Motorised vision blinds are increasingly popular. Control them via remote, smartphone app, or integrate with Alexa/Google Home. The motor allows precise positioning of the fabric layers for perfect light control at the touch of a button.

Yes, we offer moisture-resistant vision blinds perfect for bathrooms. They provide excellent privacy while still allowing natural light - ideal for Saddleworth's often grey days! The fabrics are specially treated to resist moisture and prevent mould growth.
