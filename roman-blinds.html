The Art of Roman Blind Elegance

Roman blinds represent the perfect marriage of form and function - combining the softness and luxury of curtains with the practicality and space-saving benefits of blinds. Their distinctive horizontal pleating creates beautiful, sculptural folds when raised, making them ideal for showcasing exquisite fabrics.

Particularly suited to Saddleworth's diverse interior styles, roman blinds enhance everything from the classic elegance of Diggle's period homes to the clean sophistication of modern Delph developments. They're especially popular for creating statement windows that become focal points in any room.

Roman Blinds FAQs

Everything you need to know about bespoke roman blinds

As each roman blind is handcrafted to your specifications, typical lead time is 3-4 weeks from ordering to installation. This includes fabric ordering, professional making, quality checking, and scheduling your fitting. Rush orders may be possible - please ask.

Yes, we can make roman blinds using your own fabric, though we'll need to check its suitability first. Not all fabrics work well for roman blinds - they need the right weight and drape. We have hundreds of tested fabrics to choose from if you prefer.

Roman blinds work best on windows up to 2.5m wide. For larger windows, we often recommend multiple blinds or alternative solutions. The weight of fabric on very large blinds can make them difficult to operate and may cause sagging.

Regular vacuuming with an upholstery attachment keeps them fresh. Many of our fabrics can be sponge-cleaned, and some are even machine washable (we'll advise based on your choice). Professional cleaning every few years keeps them looking pristine.

Absolutely! We offer various lining options including blackout, thermal, and standard. Blackout lining is perfect for bedrooms, while thermal lining helps with insulation - particularly useful in older Saddleworth properties with single glazing.
