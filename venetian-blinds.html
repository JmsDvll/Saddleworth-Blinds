Choose Your Perfect Venetian Blind

Each material has its own benefits - we'll help you choose the right one for your home

Real Wood Venetians

Nothing beats the natural beauty of real wood. Perfect for adding warmth and character to period properties throughout Uppermill and Lydgate. Our wooden venetians are sourced from sustainable forests and finished to perfection.

  • Natural wood grain adds character
  • Available in various stains and paints
  • Excellent insulation properties
  • Creates a premium look and feel

Faux Wood Venetians

Get the look of real wood with added practicality. Ideal for kitchens and bathrooms in Diggle and Delph homes where moisture resistance is important. They won't warp, crack or fade - perfect for our unpredictable Pennine weather!

  • Moisture and humidity resistant
  • More affordable than real wood
  • Easy to clean with just a damp cloth
  • UV resistant - won't fade or yellow

Aluminium Venetians

Sleek, modern and incredibly durable. Popular in contemporary homes across Greenfield and Dobcross. Available in a huge range of colours and finishes, from subtle metallics to bold statement shades.

  • Lightweight yet strong
  • Heat and moisture resistant
  • Huge colour range available
  • Perfect for modern interiors

Venetian Blinds FAQs

Common questions about our venetian blind range

Wooden venetian blinds offer natural beauty and warmth, perfect for living rooms and bedrooms. Faux wood blinds look similar but are moisture-resistant, making them ideal for kitchens and bathrooms. They're also more budget-friendly and won't warp in our damp Pennine climate.

Regular dusting with a microfiber cloth or duster is usually sufficient. For deeper cleaning, close the slats and wipe with a damp cloth. Aluminium blinds can be cleaned with mild soapy water. We'll provide full care instructions specific to your chosen blinds.

We offer 25mm slats for a contemporary look with better light control, and 50mm slats for a more traditional appearance with wider views when open. Your choice depends on window size and personal preference - we'll help you decide during your consultation.

Yes! We offer motorised options for venetian blinds. Control them via remote, smartphone app, or integrate with your smart home system. Particularly useful for high windows or multiple blinds that you want to operate together.

With proper care, quality venetian blinds can last 10-20 years. Aluminium blinds are particularly durable, while wooden blinds may need more care but can last just as long. All our venetian blinds come with a 5-year guarantee for peace of mind.

Absolutely! Venetian blinds work beautifully in bay windows. We can fit individual blinds to each section, allowing independent control. Many period properties in Uppermill and Diggle have bay windows, and we've fitted countless venetian blinds to them.
