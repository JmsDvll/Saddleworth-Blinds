Special Features & Options

Chainless Bottom Weights

Keep your vertical slats hanging straight without connecting chains. Safer for children and pets, and gives a cleaner appearance.

Wand Control

Control both drawing and tilting with a single wand. Popular in Saddleworth homes with children as there are no dangling cords.

Split Draw Options

Perfect for wide patio doors - blinds draw from the centre to both sides, making access easier whilst maintaining a balanced look.

Shaped Verticals

Got sloping windows or unusual shapes? We can create angled vertical blinds to fit perfectly - ideal for Saddleworth's many converted properties.
